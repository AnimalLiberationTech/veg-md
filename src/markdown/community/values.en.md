Our mission is built on **compassion and nonviolence**, fundamental principles for eliminating animal exploitation. We believe that lasting cultural change is achieved through integrity and the power of example, promoting a non-aggressive approach in all our actions.

## Our Values

* **Transparency**: We want to facilitate open and honest dialogue to build trust within the movement.  
* **Continuous evolution:** We believe in learning, researching, and adapting strategies to remain effective in a changing social context.  
* **Inclusion**: We value diversity of perspectives and experiences, but reject dogmatism and rigid labeling, opting instead for a “bigger tent.”

Our goal is to create a collaborative space that allows for constructive discussions between vegans with different ideas. Rather than imposing a single vision, we want to find the most effective methods of communication and community engagement to support animal rights.
